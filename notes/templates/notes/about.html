About Us:

Himalaya Notes is a website that focuses on providing quality notes and exam solutions to the engineering students. We have several notes and exam solutions that is mainly targeted to TU IOE students but can be useful for other university students as well.


Opening 10-12 different ppts just to study one subject and scrolling through 15-20 slides to search a particular topic not only makes your mind feel bore but also takes more time and decreases your efficiency.


In himalaya notes we have made it easier to surf through various chapters and to navigate within the chapter itself. To do that we have added: 1. a section where you can see what main topics are present within that chapter and jump right into that particuar topic
2. a section where you can see all the chapters of that particular subject. Just click on any chapter you want and you will be there


As we have just started there are very few notes and solutions on the website right now. We are working to add new materials very soon. If you actually found this website helpful share it with your friends or someone who might get benefited from this.
